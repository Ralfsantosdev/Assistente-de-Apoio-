# Assistente de Apoio Emocional - LLM Summary

## Mission
Assistente de Apoio Emocional is a specialized educational AI platform focused on anxiety management, emotional balance, and psychoeducational guidance. It provides reliable information on anxiety symptoms, crises, and treatments based on evidence-based practices (TCC/ACT).

## Core Structure
The site operates on a programmatic SEO machine with 38+ pages clustered around:
- **Pillar Content**: /ansiedade (Central hub)
- **Cluster 1: Symptoms**: Physical and emotional manifestations.
- **Cluster 2: Crises**: Management and immediate support.
- **Cluster 3: Disorders**: Generalized Anxiety (TAG), Panic, Social Anxiety.
- **Cluster 4: Treatment**: Therapy, medication protocols, natural remedies.
- **Cluster 5: Diagnosis**: Clinical criteria (DSM-5/CID-11).
- **Cluster 6: Emotional Health**: Sleep issues, morning anxiety, fatigue.

## Technical Implementation
- **Architecture**: Next.js 14 App Router.
- **Data Source**: Programmatic JSON-driven routes via `app/content.js`.
- **SEO**: Dynamic JSON-LD (MedicalWebPage, FAQPage) for all routes.
- **Sitemap**: /sitemap.js (Dynamically generated).

## AI Capabilities
The platform features an integrated AI Assistant that:
1. Guides users through breathing exercises (4-4-6 technique).
2. Performs thought organization following Cognitive-Behavioral Therapy (TCC) principles.
3. Provides psychoanalytical acolhimento and orientation.
4. Identifies patterns of anxiety symptoms through interactive dialogue.
